Live Photography
Description Of Event :

Live photography is photography contest, in which we have to capture the photos in live by following the instructions given by organisers.


RULES


A total of 30 students will be selected on the basis of First comes First basis, and participants should register through the link.


  • Live photography consists of 3 rounds.

  • The photos must be taken from the mobile camera only.

  • Participants should submit their photos in the mail id.

  • The photo must be a high resolution image and the picture should not contain any pixels divided, photo must be raw.

Round 1 : Quality of  picture:

  • The participants need to send picture along with picture details.

  • Pictures of beauty of College and Nature are allowed, No personal photos are accepted.

  • In the first round, participants are given with a description about the requirements of a photo; the time duration will run from 10:30 am to 11:30 am, that is; duration of one hour to complete the first round.

  • Participants with the picture requirements should wander only around the premises of the campus.

  • From the photos given by the participants, it will be checked and 15 participants will be selected and pushed to the second round.

  • If the participants’ picture was notselected, he/she will not get any kind of refund.

Round 2 : motive  of  picture:

  • In the second round, the duration of the second round will be from 1:30pm to 2:30pm; the rules and format of the first round will be applicable as well.

  • From the 30 students, a total of 15 students will be shortlisted for the second round.

  • The description and requirements of the photo will change as per the second round.

  • After the judgement, 5 participants out of 15 will be shortlisted and will be sent to the final round.

Round 3 : Have to explain about the picture.

  • In the third round the participants must give a detailed description about the picture submitted for the second round.

  • Participants must explain the motto and reason to capture the photo, and method of capturing the photo. 

  • The winner selected from the 5 participants will be awarded. One runner will be selected and will be considered and awarded.

CRITERIA FOR EVALUATION

  • Winners will be selected based on picture quality.

  • Composition : Clear subject in photo , good positioning.

  • Creativity : Photo should include some creativity.

  • Lighting : Lighting used properly

  • Originality and Time limit of the event

“JUDGES’ DECISION IS THE FINAL DECISION”




IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION:

Participants will be disqualified on the given basis which is mentioned below:

  • Using adult contents.

  • Using DSLR cameras

  • No editing is allowed, any type of filters are restricted, any type of applications for taking of pictures like snapchat, B612 etc are not allowed, only the inbuilt system camera app is allowed and accepted for conduction.

  • Participants cannot submit multiple photos for the judgment, Participants can submit only one photo for the first round and that photo is only is considered for the judgment.


Time duration: 3 hour
Number Of Rounds : 3
